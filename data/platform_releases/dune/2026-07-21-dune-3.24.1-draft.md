<h3>Changed</h3>
<ul>
<li>Revert sandboxing Melange rules by default in the <code>(library ..)</code> and<br>
<code>(melange.emit ..)</code> stanzas. In Melange libraries with many modules, sandbox<br>
creation / destruction dominates build time and adds significant overhead to<br>
build time (<a href="https://github.com/ocaml/dune/pull/15418" class="issue-link js-issue-link" data-error-text="Failed to load title" data-id="4808376213" data-permission-text="Title is private" data-url="https://github.com/ocaml/dune/issues/15418" data-hovercard-type="pull_request" data-hovercard-url="/ocaml/dune/pull/15418/hovercard">#15418</a>, <a href="https://github.com/anmonteiro" class="user-mention notranslate" data-hovercard-type="user" data-hovercard-url="/users/anmonteiro/hovercard" data-octo-click="hovercard-link-click" data-octo-dimensions="link_type:self">@anmonteiro</a>)</li>
</ul>
