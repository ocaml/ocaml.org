<p>Binaries and full archive are signed by the <a href="https://opam.ocaml.org/opam-dev-pubkey.pgp" rel="nofollow">opam dev team</a> (fingerprint <code>92C5 26AE 50DF 3947 0EB2 911B ED4C F1CA 67CB AA92</code>).<br>
To verify the authenticity of one of these files, run the following commands:</p>
