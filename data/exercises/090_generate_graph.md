# Statement

In a [K-regular graph](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K-regular_graph) all
nodes have a degree of K; i.e. the number of edges incident in each node
is K. How many (non-isomorphic!) 3-regular graphs with 6 nodes are
there?
